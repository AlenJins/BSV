Broseph Sikshana Vedike (BSV)

The Broseph Foundation, founded by Dushyant Dubey, is deeply committed to advancing social causes and empowering communities. One of its flagship initiatives, Broseph Sikshana Vedike (BSV), is a social education program dedicated to serving children from economically weaker sections of society.

BSV strives to provide a quality education that uplifts students both academically and socially, aiming to nurture exemplary students and compassionate human beings. Through holistic teaching models and real-world exposure, BSV inspires young minds to pursue brighter, academically enriched futures.

We believe that by fostering a strong foundation in science, critical thinking, and social empowerment, children can break free from predestined limitations and emerge as star contributors to society.

Why Volunteer

  • Make a Real Impact: Every moment you invest with us creates a tangible difference. By volunteering, you help touch and transform real lives, directly contributing to the betterment of communities.
  • Exposure to Diverse Realities: Gain a deeper understanding of society by working closely with people from different social strata. Your experiences here will broaden your perspective and enrich your worldview.
  • Practical, Hands-on Experience: Step out of theory and into practice. Volunteering with BSV provides valuable on-field exposure that nurtures practical problem-solving and leadership skills.
  • Strengthen Your Employability: Boost your professional profile by developing key employable skills such as communication, teamwork, leadership, adaptability, and time management — all highly valued in today's job market.
  • Network with Like-Minded People: Meet and collaborate with a community of passionate, driven individuals who share your commitment to making a difference. The friendships and connections you build here can last a lifetime.
  • Career Upskilling: Volunteering is a powerful tool for personal and professional growth. At BSV, you'll develop critical personal, corporate, and social skills, helping you stand out in your future career journey.

Impact

  • Tuition classes will be held for grades 8 and 9 covering the entire syllabus.
  • Tutors will contribute by teaching concepts and topics the students are struggling with as well as clearing doubts - ensuring the students are proficient by holding mock tests.
  • Volunteers are expected to tutor and contribute by facilitating smooth progression of the classes.

Program Structure

  • Classes will be held thrice a week on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays
  • Tutors can elect to volunteer on 1 or more days with activity points awarded accordingly
  • Tution classes are after classes and begin at 4:30 PM and end at 5:30
